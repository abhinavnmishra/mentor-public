Leadership Mastery: The Executive Edge



Leadership Mastery: The Executive Edge Program Objectives and Intended Outcomes The "Leadership Mastery: The Executive Edge" program is designed to elevate the leadership capabilities of executives within PhonePe, a leading organization in the banking sector. This program aims to enhance participants' strategic decision-making, communication prowess, emotional intelligence, and team dynamics. Through this program, executives will develop the skills necessary to lead with confidence, adaptability, and vision, ultimately driving organizational success and fostering a culture of excellence. Key Focus Areas and Their Relevance The program will concentrate on several pivotal focus areas crucial for executive leadership: - Executive Communication & Public Speaking: Enhancing clarity and effectiveness in communication to influence and inspire stakeholders. - Emotional Intelligence & Self-Awareness: Developing self-awareness and empathy to build stronger interpersonal relationships and improve team morale. - Team Leadership & Conflict Resolution: Equipping leaders with the tools to manage team dynamics and resolve conflicts efficiently. - Decision-Making & Strategic Thinking: Strengthening the ability to make informed, strategic decisions that align with organizational goals. - Organizational Change & Adaptability: Preparing leaders to navigate and manage change, ensuring resilience and continuity. Milestone Structure and Progression The program is structured around key milestones to ensure a systematic approach to leadership development: 1. Initial Assessment (SURVEY): Scheduled to begin on April 8, 2025, this assessment will gather baseline data on participants' current leadership capabilities. The survey will employ 360-degree feedback and self-evaluation to identify strengths and areas for improvement. 2. Developmental Milestones: Participants will engage in tailored coaching sessions focused on the identified focus areas, with periodic evaluations to track progress and adjust strategies as necessary. 3. Final Review and Next Steps: Concluding the program, a comprehensive evaluation will summarize achievements and outline ongoing development opportunities. Development Approach and Methodology Under the guidance of John Carter, a seasoned leadership coach, the program leverages evidence-based methodologies such as the DISC personality model and behavioral coaching frameworks. John's approach ensures that each participant receives a personalized, high-impact growth plan. By translating psychological insights into actionable strategies, the program emphasizes real-world application and measurable progress. This program represents a unique opportunity for PhonePe executives to refine their leadership skills, enhance their strategic impact, and contribute to the organization's sustained success. // Suggestions for the Trainer - Consider integrating specific case studies or real-world scenarios from the banking sector to enhance contextual relevance. - Encourage participants to set personal leadership goals at the start of the program to foster a sense of ownership and motivation. - Provide opportunities for peer-to-peer learning and networking to strengthen the leadership community within PhonePe.
